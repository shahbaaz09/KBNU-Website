Admissions 2025

Explore eligibility, process and programmes offered for UG, PG and Ph.D admissions.

Admission Overview

Khaja Bandanawaz University admits students to its Undergraduate (UG), Postgraduate (PG) and Doctoral (Ph.D) programmes based on eligibility, merit and entrance examinations wherever applicable. Candidates must follow the admission procedure as notified by the University for the respective academic year.

Undergraduate (UG) Admissions

Eligibility

10+2 / PUC or equivalent qualification from a recognized board with required subject combinations based on the programme.

Programmes Offered

  • MBBS
  • B.Tech / B.E
  • B.Sc Programmes
  • B.Com
  • BBA
  • BA (Languages / Humanities / Psychology)
  • LL.B & Integrated Law
  • B.Ed

Application Process

Submit application form along with required documents. Visit Downloads section for the application form.

Postgraduate (PG) Admissions

Eligibility

A bachelor’s degree in the relevant discipline with minimum required marks.

Programmes Offered

  • M.D / M.S Medical Specializations
  • M.Tech (Engineering)
  • M.Sc (Sciences)
  • M.A (Arts / Humanities)
  • M.Com
  • MBA

Application Process

Submit PG application form along with certificates and entrance exam scorecards (if applicable).

Doctoral (Ph.D) Admissions

Eligibility

Master’s degree in the relevant discipline with minimum 55% marks (or as per UGC norms).

Specializations Offered

  • Medical Sciences
  • Engineering & Technology
  • Sciences (Biotech, Chemistry, Physics, etc.)
  • Languages & Humanities
  • Commerce & Management

Admission Process

Entrance test + interview as per University Ph.D admission regulations. Shortlisted candidates will be notified.
