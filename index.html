About KBNU

Khaja Bandanawaz University is a center of higher learning committed to academic excellence, ethical values and social responsibility. The University offers a wide spectrum of programmes across Engineering, Medicine, Arts & Science, Commerce, Management and allied disciplines.

Administration

Syed Shah Muhammed Al Hussaini (RH)

Founder – Khaja Education Society

An Indian Sufi scholar, educational philanthropist, and social worker who laid the foundation of modern education in Kalaburagi by establishing the first girls’ school in 1958.

Dr. Syed Shah Khusro Hussaini (RH)

Founder Chancellor – KBNU

A visionary educational leader whose contributions shaped the academic, cultural, and spiritual legacy of Khaja Bandanawaz institutions.

Syed Muhammad Ali Al Hussaini

Chancellor – KBNU

Providing strategic direction and leadership for the university’s growth and development. He is the elected Chairman of the Karnataka State Waqf Board and Sajjada Nasheen, Dargah Hazrat Khaja Bandanawaz Gesudaraz (R.H), Gulbarga.
